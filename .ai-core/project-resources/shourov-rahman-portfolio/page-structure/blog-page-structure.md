# Blog Page Structure (`/blog`)

## Overall Purpose
Content marketing hub for SEO, thought leadership, and lead nurturing. Target long-tail keywords and demonstrate expertise in ecommerce optimization, tracking, and paid advertising.

---

## Blog Index Page Structure (`/blog`)

### 1. Hero Section
**Purpose:** Establish content hub

**Elements:**
- H1 headline (content hub positioning)
- Subheadline (value and topics covered)
- Breadcrumb navigation: Home > Blog
- Optional: Search bar for blog content
- Optional: Newsletter signup CTA: "Get weekly ecommerce tips"

**Layout:**
- Centered content
- Reduced hero height
- Clean, editorial design
- Search bar prominently placed

---

### 2. Featured Post Section
**Purpose:** Highlight top content

**Elements:**
- H2: "Featured Article"
- Large featured post card:
  - Featured image (large)
  - Category badge (e.g., "Conversion Optimization")
  - Post title (H3)
  - Excerpt (2-3 sentences)
  - Author info: Shourov Rahman + publish date
  - Read time: "X min read"
  - "Read more" CTA

**Layout:**
- Full-width or large card
- Image left, content right (desktop)
- Stacked (mobile)
- Highlighted with border or background color

---

### 3. Category Filter Section
**Purpose:** Content navigation

**Elements:**
- Filter tabs/buttons:
  - All Posts
  - Conversion Optimization
  - Tracking Setup
  - Paid Advertising
  - Ecommerce Strategy
  - Tools & Resources

**Layout:**
- Horizontal tab bar (desktop)
- Dropdown or horizontal scroll (mobile)
- Active category highlighted
- Sticky on scroll (optional)

---

### 4. Recent Posts Grid
**Purpose:** Display all blog content

**Elements:**
- Grid of blog post cards (9-12 per page)
- Each card:
  - Featured image
  - Category badge
  - Post title (H3)
  - Excerpt (1-2 sentences)
  - Author info: Shourov Rahman + publish date
  - Read time: "X min read"
  - "Read more" link

**Layout:**
- 3-column grid (desktop)
- 2-column grid (tablet)
- 1-column (mobile)
- Equal height cards
- Hover effects on cards
- Lazy loading for images

---

### 5. Pagination Section
**Purpose:** Navigate through posts

**Elements:**
- Pagination controls:
  - "← Previous" button
  - Page numbers (1, 2, 3, ... 10)
  - "Next →" button
- OR "Load More" button for infinite scroll

**Layout:**
- Centered controls
- Clear active page indicator
- Mobile-friendly button sizes

---

### 6. Footer
(Same as homepage footer)

## Individual Blog Post Structure (`/blog/[slug]`)

### Purpose
Comprehensive article page optimized for SEO, engagement, and lead conversion.

---

### Blog Post Page Sections:

#### 1. Header Section
**Elements:**
- Breadcrumb: Home > Blog > [Category] > [Post Title]
- Category badge
- H1: Post title
- Author info:
  - Small author photo (Shourov Rahman)
  - Author name
  - Publish date
  - Last updated date (if applicable)
  - Read time: "X min read"
- Social share buttons:
  - Twitter/X
  - LinkedIn
  - Facebook
  - Copy link

**Layout:**
- Centered content
- Clean typography
- Author info as horizontal bar
- Share buttons prominently placed

---

#### 2. Featured Image Section
**Elements:**
- Large featured image relevant to post topic
- Optional: Image caption or credit

**Layout:**
- Full-width or contained width
- Optimized for LCP (Largest Contentful Paint)
- Alt text for SEO

---

#### 3. Table of Contents (For Long Posts)
**Purpose:** Improve scannability and engagement

**Elements:**
- "In This Article:" heading
- Clickable list of H2 headings
- Smooth scroll to sections
- Optional: Sticky TOC sidebar (desktop)

**Layout:**
- Bordered box before main content
- Collapsible on mobile
- Sticky sidebar (desktop, optional)

---

#### 4. Main Content Section
**Purpose:** Deliver value and establish expertise

**Elements:**
- Body content (1,500-3,000 words):
  - Introduction (problem/hook)
  - Detailed sections (H2, H3 headings)
  - Actionable steps or insights
  - Examples and case studies
  - Screenshots or diagrams
  - Code snippets (if technical)
  - Bullet points and numbered lists
  - Conclusion with key takeaways
  
- Content formatting:
  - Clear heading hierarchy (H2, H3, H4)
  - Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
  - Bold for emphasis
  - Inline links to related posts or resources
  - Callout boxes for important points
  - Images/screenshots throughout
  - Video embeds (if applicable)

**Layout:**
- Single column, max-width for readability (700-800px)
- Ample line height and spacing
- Clean, distraction-free design
- Mobile-optimized images

---

#### 5. Embedded CTA (Single, Optional)
**Purpose:** Convert engaged readers without being pushy

**Elements:**
- 1 contextual CTA placed after 60% of content:
  - Relevant to content topic
  - Example: "Need help implementing this? Book a free consultation."
  - Button: "Get Expert Help" or similar
  - Only include if highly relevant to post topic

**Layout:**
- Bordered box or subtle background
- Centered within content flow
- Non-disruptive placement

---

#### 6. Key Takeaways Section
**Purpose:** Summarize value

**Elements:**
- H2: "Key Takeaways"
- 3-5 bullet points summarizing main insights
- Actionable next steps

**Layout:**
- Highlighted box
- Bullet points or numbered list
- Positioned before conclusion or as conclusion

---

#### 7. Author Bio Section
**Purpose:** Build authority and connection

**Elements:**
- Author photo (Shourov Rahman)
- H3: "About Shourov Rahman"
- Bio (3-4 sentences):
  - Expertise
  - Credentials
  - Value proposition
- Links:
  - "View Portfolio" → /portfolio
  - "Book a Call" → /contact or calendar
  - "Connect on LinkedIn" → LinkedIn profile

**Layout:**
- Bordered box or section divider
- Photo left, text right
- Prominent CTAs

---

#### 8. Related Posts Section
**Purpose:** Keep readers engaged

**Elements:**
- H2: "Related Articles"
- 3-4 related post cards:
  - Thumbnail image
  - Category badge
  - Post title
  - Excerpt (1 sentence)
  - "Read more" link
- Selection criteria:
  - Same category, or
  - Related tags, or
  - Similar topic

**Layout:**
- 3-column grid (desktop)
- Horizontal scroll or stacked (mobile)
- Cards with hover effects

---

#### 9. Newsletter CTA Section
**Purpose:** Convert readers to subscribers

**Elements:**
- H2: "Get More Insights Like This"
- Subheading: "Join XX+ ecommerce professionals getting weekly tips"
- Email signup form:
  - Email input
  - "Subscribe" button
- Privacy note

**Layout:**
- Full-width section
- Contrasting background
- Large, prominent form

---

#### 10. Final CTA Section
**Purpose:** Last conversion opportunity

**Elements:**
- Heading: "Ready to Optimize Your Ecommerce Business?"
- Subheading: "Let's discuss how I can help you achieve your goals"
- CTAs:
  - Primary: "Schedule a Call"
  - Secondary: "View Services" → /services

**Layout:**
- Centered content
- Contrasting background
- Large CTA buttons

---

#### 11. Footer
(Same as homepage footer)

## SEO Optimization

### Blog Index Page
- **Title Tag:** "Ecommerce Blog: Conversion, Tracking & Paid Ads Insights | Shourov Rahman"
- **Meta Description:** "Expert insights on ecommerce conversion optimization, Meta Pixel setup, and profitable Facebook/Google Ads. Actionable guides for DTC brands and agencies."
- **H1:** "Ecommerce Insights & Strategies"
- **Schema:** Blog, CollectionPage

### Individual Blog Posts
- **Title Tag:** "[Post Title] | Shourov Rahman"
  - Example: "How to Set Up Meta Pixel on Shopify (Step-by-Step) | Shourov Rahman"
- **Meta Description:** [Summary of post value, 150-160 chars]
- **H1:** [Post Title]
- **Schema:** Article, BlogPosting
- **Internal Links:** 3-5 links to related posts or service pages
- **External Links:** 2-3 authoritative sources

---

## Content Guidelines

### Writing Style
- **Conversational but professional**
- **Actionable:** Every post should have clear takeaways
- **Scannable:** Short paragraphs, subheadings, bullet points
- **Visual:** Include screenshots, diagrams, examples
- **SEO-optimized:** Target keywords naturally, not forced

### Post Length
- **Pillar posts:** 2,500-3,500 words
- **Standard posts:** 1,500-2,500 words
- **Quick guides:** 800-1,500 words

### Publishing Frequency (Post-Launch)
- **MVP:** 5-10 existing posts at launch
- **Ongoing:** 1-2 posts per week
- **Goal:** 50+ posts within 6 months

---

## Technical Requirements

### Performance
- Fast page load (target <2s)
- Lazy load images
- Optimized featured images (WebP)
- Minimal JavaScript

### Accessibility
- Proper heading hierarchy
- Alt text for all images
- High contrast text
- Keyboard navigation

### Analytics
Track events:
- Blog page views
- Read time/scroll depth
- CTA clicks
- Newsletter signups
- Related post clicks

---

## Mobile Considerations
- Single column layout
- Larger font sizes for readability
- Touch-friendly CTAs
- Simplified navigation
- Remove or condense sidebar content

---

## Key Conversion Elements
- **Blog Index CTAs:** None (newsletter in hero only - removed sidebar)
- **Individual Post CTAs:** 4 total (1 optional embedded, Author bio, Newsletter, Final CTA)
- **Internal Linking:** Every post links to 2-3 service pages
- **Email Capture:** Newsletter signup in hero (index) and dedicated section (posts)
- **Section count:** Index: 6 sections | Individual Post: 11 sections (down from 12)
- **UX improvements:** Removed redundant newsletter CTA from index (kept in hero), reduced embedded CTAs from 2-3 to 1 optional (less sales-y), removed comments section (cleaner, less maintenance, encourages social engagement instead)
