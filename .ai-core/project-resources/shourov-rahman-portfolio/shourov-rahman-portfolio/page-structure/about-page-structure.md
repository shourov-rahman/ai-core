# About Page Structure (`/about`)

## Overall Purpose
Humanize the brand, establish credibility, and differentiate from competitors. Build trust through personal story and demonstrate why you're uniquely qualified to help ecommerce brands.

---

## Section Flow

### 1. Hero Section
**Purpose:** Personal introduction and immediate credibility

**Elements:**
- Professional headshot (large, prominent)
- H1 headline (personal introduction)
- Subheadline (value proposition from expert's perspective)
- Breadcrumb navigation: Home > About
- Key credentials bar:
  - Shopify Partner
  - Meta Blueprint Certified
  - Google Ads Certified
  - X+ years experience
  - XX+ brands served

**Layout:**
- Split layout: Headshot left (40%), content right (60%)
- Centered content on mobile
- Credentials as horizontal icon badges
- Professional, clean design

---

### 2. Why I'm Different Section
**Purpose:** Differentiation and competitive positioning

**Elements:**
- H2 (differentiation-focused heading)
- 4-6 differentiation points, each with:
  - Icon or visual element
  - Bold heading
  - 2-3 sentence explanation
  
  **Example points:**
  1. **Technical + Marketing Hybrid**
     - Unlike pure designers or pure marketers, I bridge both worlds
  2. **Results-Focused Approach**
     - I measure success in ROAS, CVR, and revenue—not just deliverables
  3. **Ecommerce Specialization**
     - 100% focused on DTC brands, not generic web development
  4. **Transparent Process**
     - Clear timelines, regular updates, no black box
  5. **Certified Expertise**
     - Meta Blueprint, Google Ads, Shopify Partner credentials
  6. **White-Label Partnership**
     - Seamless collaboration with agencies, no ego

**Layout:**
- 2-column grid (desktop)
- Stacked cards (mobile)
- Icons aligned left
- Cards with subtle background

---

### 3. Expertise & Credentials Section
**Purpose:** Demonstrate technical competence and third-party validation

**Elements:**
- H2: "Expertise & Credentials"
- Split layout:
  
  **Left Column: Skills**
  - Three skill categories:
    
    **Store Development**
    - Shopify (Liquid, theme customization)
    - WooCommerce (WordPress, PHP)
    - Landing page builders (GemPages, Replo)
    - UX/UI design principles
    - Conversion rate optimization
    
    **Paid Advertising**
    - Facebook/Instagram Ads Manager
    - Google Ads (Search, Shopping, Display)
    - Campaign strategy & scaling
    - Creative testing frameworks
    - Audience research and targeting
    
    **Tracking & Analytics**
    - Meta Pixel & CAPI
    - Google Analytics 4
    - Google Tag Manager
    - Server-side tracking
    - Attribution modeling
  
  **Right Column: Certifications & Partnerships**
  - Grid of certification badges/logos:
    - Shopify Partner
    - Meta Blueprint Certified
    - Google Ads Certified
    - [Any other relevant certifications]
  - Each with:
    - Badge/logo
    - Certification name
    - Year obtained (optional)

**Layout:**
- 2-column layout: Skills (60%) | Certifications (40%)
- Skills grouped by category with checkmarks
- Official certification logos/badges
- Stacked on mobile (skills first, then certifications)

---

### 4. Work Philosophy Section
**Purpose:** Communicate values and approach

**Elements:**
- H2: "How I Work"
- 3 core philosophy principles:
  
  1. **Results Over Vanity Metrics**
     - Focus on ROAS, CVR, revenue—not just traffic or likes
  
  2. **Data-Driven Decisions**
     - Every change backed by analytics and testing
  
  3. **Transparent Communication**
     - Regular updates, clear timelines, honest feedback

**Layout:**
- Horizontal 3-column grid
- Large icons for each principle
- 1-2 sentences max per principle
- Clean, scannable design

---

### 5. Client Testimonials Section
**Purpose:** Third-party validation of character and work quality

**Elements:**
- H2: "What Clients Say"
- 4-6 testimonials specifically about working with you:
  - Focus on: Communication, expertise, results, reliability
- Each testimonial:
  - Client quote (2-3 sentences)
  - Client name
  - Company
  - Photo (if available)

**Layout:**
- 2-column grid (desktop)
- Slider/carousel (mobile)
- Highlighted quotes
- Client info below

---

### 6. CTA Section
**Purpose:** Convert about page visitors

**Elements:**
- Heading: "Ready to Grow Your Ecommerce Business?"
- Subheadline: "Let's discuss how I can help you achieve your revenue goals"
- Two CTAs:
  - Primary: "Schedule a Strategy Call"
  - Secondary: "View My Work" → /portfolio
- Trust element: "Free 30-minute consultation with actionable insights"

**Layout:**
- Centered content
- Contrasting background
- Large CTA buttons
- Full-width section

---

### 7. Footer
(Same as homepage footer)

## SEO Optimization
- **Title Tag:** "About Shourov Rahman | Ecommerce Developer & Paid Ads Specialist"
- **Meta Description:** "Hi, I'm Shourov Rahman—a Shopify developer, Facebook/Google Ads specialist, and conversion tracking expert helping ecommerce brands grow profitably. Based in Bangladesh, serving global clients."
- **H1:** "Hi, I'm Shourov Rahman"
- **Schema:** Person, ProfilePage

## Mobile Considerations
- Stack split layouts to single column
- Collapsible skill sections
- Simplified timeline view
- Touch-friendly contact buttons
- Reduced image sizes

## Content Tone Guidelines
- **First person:** Use "I" and "my" throughout
- **Conversational:** Write like you're talking to a potential client
- **Authentic:** Be genuine, avoid corporate speak
- **Confident but humble:** Demonstrate expertise without arrogance
- **Story-driven:** Use narrative elements to engage

## Key Conversion Elements
- **CTA Count:** 2 (Hero, Final CTA)
- **Trust Indicators:** 3 sections (Expertise & Credentials combined, Testimonials, Philosophy)
- **Differentiation:** 2 sections (Why Different, Work Philosophy)
- **Section count:** 7 sections (Hero, Why Different, Expertise & Credentials, Philosophy, Testimonials, CTA, Footer)
- **UX improvements:** Merged Skills and Certifications into single cohesive section for better scanning, reduced Work Philosophy from 5 to 3 core principles for stronger impact, more focused on credentials and expertise

## Photo/Visual Elements Needed
- Professional headshot (hero)
- Optional: Casual professional photo (story section)
- Optional: Workspace or environment photo
- Optional: Personal interest photos
- Certification badges/logos
- Icons for skills and differentiation points

## Content Length
- Target: 1,200-1,800 words total
- Focus: Quality storytelling over quantity
- Balance: Professional expertise + personal connection
