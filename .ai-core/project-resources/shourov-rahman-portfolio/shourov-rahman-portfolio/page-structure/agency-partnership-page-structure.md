# Agency Partnership Page Structure (`/agency-partnership`)

## Overall Purpose
Dedicated landing page targeting ICP 2 (marketing agencies) seeking white-label ecommerce services. Emphasize reliability, quality, and partnership benefits.

---

## Section Flow

### 1. Hero Section
**Purpose:** Immediate relevance for agency visitors

**Elements:**
- H1 headline (white-label B2B positioning)
- Subheadline (reliability and service promise)
- Breadcrumb navigation: Home > Agency Partnership
- Primary CTA: "Schedule a Partnership Call"
- Trust indicators:
  - X+ agencies partnered with
  - XX+ projects delivered
  - 100% on-time delivery rate
  - Average project rating: 4.9/5

**Layout:**
- Centered content
- Full viewport height
- Professional, B2B-focused design
- Trust indicators as stat bar below CTAs

---

### 2. Solutions for Agencies Section
**Purpose:** Address pain points with integrated solutions

**Elements:**
- H2: "How We Solve Your Agency Challenges"
- 4 problem → solution → benefit cards:
  
  **1. Store Development Expertise**
  - Problem: "No in-house Shopify specialist, hiring is expensive"
  - Solution: "Expert Shopify, WooCommerce, and landing page development on demand"
  - Services: Store design, WooCommerce sites, GemPages/Replo landing pages, migrations, audits
  - Benefit: Expand service offerings without overhead
  
  **2. Paid Advertising Support**
  - Problem: "Clients need full-funnel services but you lack ad expertise"
  - Solution: "Facebook/Instagram Ads, Google Ads setup and management"
  - Services: Campaign setup, creative testing, retargeting, ROAS optimization
  - Benefit: Offer complete marketing solutions
  
  **3. Tracking & Technical Setup**
  - Problem: "Meta Pixel, CAPI, GA4 setup is complex and time-consuming"
  - Solution: "Bulletproof tracking setup and conversion optimization"
  - Services: Meta Pixel, CAPI, GA4, event tracking, attribution reporting
  - Benefit: Better client results, higher retention
  
  **4. Reliable Delivery Partner**
  - Problem: "Unreliable freelancers, delivery bottlenecks, quality issues"
  - Solution: "Clear timelines, regular updates, portfolio-quality work"
  - Services: White-label project delivery, transparent communication, on-time completion
  - Benefit: Happy clients, stronger reputation

**Layout:**
- 2x2 grid (desktop)
- Stacked cards (mobile)
- Each card shows: Problem header → Solution → Services list → Benefit callout
- Visual distinction between problem (orange) and benefit (green)

---

### 3. How Partnership Works Section
**Purpose:** Clarify collaboration process

**Elements:**
- H2: "How We Work Together"
- 6-step process:
  
  1. **You Connect with Client**
     - You sell the project under your agency brand
     - Collect requirements and budget
  
  2. **You Brief Me**
     - Share project scope via email or call
     - I provide timeline and quote
  
  3. **I Deliver**
     - Work is completed on schedule
     - Regular progress updates to you
     - All assets delivered in white-label format
  
  4. **You Review**
     - Quality check before client delivery
     - Request any revisions needed
     - Final approval
  
  5. **You Present to Client**
     - Deliver work under your agency brand
     - I remain completely behind the scenes
  
  6. **Client is Happy**
     - Your agency gets credit
     - Client retention improves
     - We repeat for next project

**Layout:**
- Vertical timeline or numbered list
- Icons for each step
- Clear "You / Me / Client" designations
- Connecting lines between steps

---

### 4. Partnership Benefits Section
**Purpose:** Reinforce value proposition

**Elements:**
- H2: "Why Agencies Choose Me"
- 4 benefit cards:
  
  1. **100% White-Label**
     - No branding, no contact with your clients
     - You get full credit
  
  2. **Transparent Pricing**
     - Clear quotes upfront
     - No hidden fees
     - Agency-friendly discounts
  
  3. **Reliable Communication**
     - Regular progress updates
     - Same-day responses
     - Proactive problem-solving
  
  4. **Portfolio-Quality Work**
     - Work that enhances your reputation
     - Client-ready deliverables
     - Professional documentation

**Layout:**
- 2x2 grid (desktop)
- Stacked cards (mobile)
- Icons for each benefit
- Green checkmarks or badges

---

### 5. Testimonials from Agency Partners Section
**Purpose:** Social proof from target ICP

**Elements:**
- H2: "What Agency Partners Say"
- 4-6 testimonials from agency owners/directors:
  - Focus on: Reliability, communication, quality, partnership value
- Each testimonial:
  - Quote (2-3 sentences)
  - Name and title
  - Agency name (or "Major LA-based agency" if confidential)
  - Photo (if available)

**Layout:**
- 2-column grid (desktop)
- Slider (mobile)
- Highlighted quote formatting
- Professional headshots if available

---

### 6. Pricing for Agencies Section
**Purpose:** Address cost questions transparently

**Elements:**
- H2: "Partnership Pricing"
- Pricing structure explanation:
  - **Project-Based:** $X,XXX-$XX,XXX (depending on scope)
  - **Monthly Retainer:** $X,XXX-$X,XXX/month (dedicated capacity)
  - **Hourly Rate:** $XX-$XXX/hour (for smaller tasks)
  - **Agency Discount:** 15-25% off standard rates
  
- Pricing factors:
  - Complexity of project
  - Timeline requirements
  - Volume commitment
  - Ongoing partnership vs. one-off

- CTA: "Request a Custom Quote"

**Layout:**
- Centered content
- Table or card layout for pricing tiers
- Highlighted agency discount
- Transparent, professional presentation

---

### 7. Partnership Inquiry Form Section
**Purpose:** Capture qualified agency leads with dedicated form

**Elements:**
- Form fields:
  - Agency Name*
  - Your Name*
  - Your Role* (Owner, Director, Account Manager, etc.)
  - Email*
  - Phone
  - Agency Website
  - Team Size (dropdown): 1-5, 6-10, 11-20, 21-50, 50+
  - Looking For (checkboxes): Shopify Dev, WooCommerce, Landing Pages, Tracking Setup, Paid Ads Support
  - Monthly Project Volume: 1-2, 3-5, 6-10, 10+
  - Preferred Partnership Type: Project-based, Retainer, Hybrid, Not sure
  - Message*
  - [ ] Privacy Policy agreement*
  
- Submit: "Send Partnership Inquiry"

**Layout:**
- Single column form
- Grouped sections
- Inline validation
- Mobile-optimized

## SEO Optimization
- **Title Tag:** "White-Label Ecommerce Services for Agencies | Shopify & Paid Ads Partner"
- **Meta Description:** "Reliable white-label Shopify development, Facebook/Google Ads, and tracking setup for marketing agencies. On-time delivery, portfolio-quality work, 100% behind the scenes."
- **H1:** "White-Label Ecommerce Services for Growing Agencies"
- **Target Keywords:** White-label Shopify development, ecommerce agency partner, white-label paid ads

## Mobile Considerations
- Condensed process flows
- Simplified pricing tables
- Touch-friendly form inputs
- Collapsible FAQ sections

## Content Tone
- **Professional B2B:** More formal than homepage
- **Partnership-focused:** "We" language
- **Results-oriented:** Focus on agency outcomes
- **Transparent:** Clear pricing and processes

## Key Conversion Elements
- **CTA Count:** 4 (Hero, After testimonials, Pricing, Form)
- **Trust Indicators:** Testimonials, delivery stats, transparent pricing
- **Risk Reducers:** NDA mention, quality guarantee, transparent pricing, trial project option
- **Differentiation:** 100% white-label, on-time delivery, transparent communication
- **Section count:** 8 sections (Hero, Solutions for Agencies, Partnership Process, Benefits, Testimonials, Pricing, Form, Footer)
- **UX improvements:** Merged challenges and services into cohesive "Solutions" section, reduced benefits from 6 to 4 core differentiators, removed case studies section (can add post-launch if needed), consolidated FAQ into form section context, cleaner narrative flow

## Performance Priorities
- Fast page load (professional presentation)
- Optimized form for lead capture  
- Clear, scannable content
- Mobile-responsive design
