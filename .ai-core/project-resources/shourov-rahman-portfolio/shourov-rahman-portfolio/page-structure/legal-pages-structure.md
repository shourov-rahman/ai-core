# Privacy Policy & Terms of Service Page Structures

## Privacy Policy Page Structure (`/privacy`)

### Overall Purpose
Legal compliance page explaining how user data is collected, used, and protected. Required for GDPR, email marketing, and form submissions.

---

### Section Flow

#### 1. Header Section
**Elements:**
- Breadcrumb: Home > Privacy Policy
- H1: "Privacy Policy"
- Last updated date: "Last Updated: [Date]"
- Brief intro: "This Privacy Policy explains how Shourov Rahman ("I," "me," or "my") collects, uses, and protects your personal information."

**Layout:**
- Simple header
- Professional, legal tone
- Clear last updated date

---

#### 2. Table of Contents
**Purpose:** Easy navigation for long legal content

**Elements:**
- Clickable list of sections:
  1. Information We Collect
  2. How We Use Your Information
  3. Data Storage and Security
  4. Third-Party Services
  5. Cookies and Tracking
  6. Your Rights
  7. Children's Privacy
  8. Changes to This Policy
  9. Contact Information

**Layout:**
- Numbered list
- Anchor links to sections
- Sticky sidebar (desktop, optional)

---

#### 3. Main Content Sections

**Section 1: Information We Collect**
- Personal information (name, email, business info from forms)
- Automatically collected data (IP address, browser, device)
- Cookies and tracking technologies

**Section 2: How We Use Your Information**
- Responding to inquiries
- Providing services
- Sending newsletters (with consent)
- Analytics and website improvement
- Legal compliance

**Section 3: Data Storage and Security**
- Where data is stored
- Security measures taken
- Data retention period
- Disclosure: No 100% guarantee of security

**Section 4: Third-Party Services**
- List of third-party services used:
  - Google Analytics
  - Meta Pixel
  - Email marketing platform (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, etc.)
  - Calendar booking (Calendly, Cal.com)
  - Hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages)
- Links to their privacy policies

**Section 5: Cookies and Tracking**
- What cookies are used
- Purpose of cookies (analytics, advertising, functionality)
- How to disable cookies
- Impact of disabling cookies

**Section 6: Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)**
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- How to exercise these rights

**Section 7: Children's Privacy**
- Statement that services are not intended for children under 13/16
- No knowing collection of children's data

**Section 8: Changes to This Policy**
- Right to update policy
- How users will be notified (email, website notice)
- Effective date of changes

**Section 9: Contact Information**
- How to contact with privacy questions
- Email: your@email.com
- Response timeframe

**Layout:**
- Single column text
- Clear H2 for each main section
- H3 for subsections
- Bullet points for lists
- Professional, readable formatting
- Max-width for readability (700-800px)

## Terms of Service Page Structure (`/terms`)

### Overall Purpose
Legal protection outlining terms of website use and service engagement. Defines rights, responsibilities, and limitations.

---

### Section Flow

#### 1. Header Section
**Elements:**
- Breadcrumb: Home > Terms of Service
- H1: "Terms of Service"
- Last updated date: "Last Updated: [Date]"
- Brief intro: "By accessing or using this website, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service."

**Layout:**
- Simple header
- Clear acceptance statement
- Professional tone

---

#### 2. Table of Contents
**Elements:**
- Clickable list of sections:
  1. Acceptance of Terms
  2. Services Description
  3. User Obligations
  4. Intellectual Property
  5. Disclaimer of Warranties
  6. Limitation of Liability
  7. Indemnification
  8. Termination
  9. Governing Law
  10. Changes to Terms
  11. Contact Information

**Layout:**
- Numbered list
- Anchor links

---

#### 3. Main Content Sections

**Section 1: Acceptance of Terms**
- Agreement to terms by using website
- Age requirement (18+)
- Right to refuse service

**Section 2: Services Description**
- General overview of services offered
- Not a guarantee of specific results
- Services provided "as is"

**Section 3: User Obligations**
- Accurate information in forms
- Prohibited uses of website (spam, hacking, etc.)
- Responsibility for account security

**Section 4: Intellectual Property**
- Copyright of website content (yours)
- Trademark rights
- User-generated content ownership (if applicable)
- Restrictions on content use

**Section 5: Disclaimer of Warranties**
- Website provided "as is"
- No warranty of uninterrupted service
- No guarantee of specific business results
- Accuracy of information disclaimer

**Section 6: Limitation of Liability**
- Limit on damages (to extent permitted by law)
- No liability for indirect or consequential damages
- Maximum liability amount (if any)
- Exceptions required by law

**Section 7: Indemnification**
- User agrees to indemnify you from claims
- Related to their use of website or services

**Section 8: Termination**
- Right to terminate access
- User's right to stop using services
- Effect of termination

**Section 9: Governing Law**
- Which jurisdiction's laws apply
- Dispute resolution method (arbitration, courts)
- Venue for legal proceedings

**Section 10: Changes to Terms**
- Right to modify terms
- How users will be notified
- Continued use = acceptance

**Section 11: Contact Information**
- How to contact with questions
- Email address
- Physical address (if applicable)

**Layout:**
- Single column text
- Clear section headings
- Legal but readable language
- Max-width for readability

## Shared Implementation Notes

### Legal Disclaimer
**Important:** These structures are templates. You should:
1. Consult a lawyer for your jurisdiction
2. Customize to your specific business practices
3. Update when services or tools change
4. Review annually

### Tone Guidelines
- **Professional but understandable:** Legal but not overly complex
- **Transparent:** Clear about data practices
- **User-friendly:** Break up dense text, use plain language where possible

### Required Compliance
Depending on your audience geography:
- **GDPR** (EU visitors): Cookie consent, data rights, clear consent for marketing
- **CCPA** (California visitors): Right to know, delete, opt-out
- **CAN-SPAM** (Email marketing): Unsubscribe mechanism, physical address

### Cookie Consent Banner
If required (GDPR):
- Appears on first visit
- Options: Accept All, Reject Optional, Customize
- Cookie categories:
  - Necessary (always on)
  - Analytics (optional)
  - Marketing (optional)
- Link to full Privacy Policy
- Preference saved in cookie/local storage

## Key Elements

### Privacy Policy
- **Compliance focus:** GDPR, data protection
- **User rights:** Clear explanation of privacy rights
- **Third-party transparency:** All tracking/tools disclosed
- **Contact info:** Easy way to exercise rights

### Terms of Service
- **Legal protection:** Limitation of liability, disclaimers
- **Clear terms:** Service description, user obligations
- **Dispute resolution:** Governing law, venue
- **Contact info:** Legal inquiries address
