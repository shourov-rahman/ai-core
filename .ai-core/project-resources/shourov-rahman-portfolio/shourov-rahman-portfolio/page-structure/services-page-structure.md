# Services Page Structure (`/services`)

## Overall Purpose
Detailed service descriptions for visitors who want to understand specific offerings. Targets all ICPs with emphasis on business outcomes and expertise demonstration.

---

## Section Flow

### 1. Hero Section
**Purpose:** Immediate context and navigation

**Elements:**
- H1 headline (benefit-driven service positioning)
- Subheadline (value proposition summary)
- Breadcrumb navigation: Home > Services
- Anchor navigation to jump to specific service sections:
  - "Store Development"
  - "Paid Advertising"
  - "Tracking & Analytics"

**Layout:**
- Centered content
- Reduced height compared to homepage hero
- Clean, professional design
- Sticky anchor navigation (desktop)

## Mobile Considerations
- Sticky anchor navigation for quick service jumping
- Collapsible service breakdowns to reduce scroll
- Touch-friendly filter buttons
- Simplified package comparison cards

## Key Conversion Points
- **CTA Count:** 4 (Service sections x3 + Final CTA)
- **CTA Spacing:** Every 1.5 sections
- **Primary Action:** Book consultation or send message
- **Section count:** 6 sections (Hero, 3 Service Details, Testimonials, Final CTA)
- **UX improvements:** Removed redundant overview grid, eliminated mini case studies (redirect to /portfolio), removed service packages (focus on custom solutions), removed agency callout (handled on dedicated page), simplified testimonials for faster scanning
